MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement (the "Agreement") is made and effective on April 17, 2026.

BETWEEN: DXP Enterprises, Inc. dba PumpWorks (Hereafter referred to as the "Client"), a corporate entity organized and existing under the laws of the State of Texas with its head office located at:

5301 Hollister Street, Houston, TX 77040

AND: Softway Solutions Inc. dba Culture+ (a Texas corporation) (Hereafter referred to as "Softway"), a company organized and existing under the laws of the State of Texas, with its head office located at:

1801 Main St. Houston, TX 77002

1. Client obligations: materials

Client shall cooperate and make available to Softway information, materials, facilities and equipment. The Client shall provide Softway within a reasonable amount of time all information, documentation, images, code or files requested by Softway and necessary for Softway to perform this Agreement.

2. Force majeure

Any delay or nonperformance of any provision of this Agreement caused by conditions beyond the reasonable control of the performing party shall not constitute a breach of this Agreement.

3. Confidentiality

During the term of this Agreement and for 1 year 2 years afterward, Softway will use all Reasonable care to prevent the unauthorized use or dissemination of Client's confidential information.

⚠️ Client Comment: PumpWorks legal requires 2-year confidentiality period to align with their standard vendor agreements.

4. Employee and Contractor NDA's

Softway shall have in place a non-disclosure agreement with each employee and contractor who contributes work and services under this Agreement.

5. Use of the Services.

Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, Softway grants to Client a limited non-exclusive, non-transferable, non-sublicensable right during the term of each Statement of Work to use the Services set forth therein.

6. Payment

All relevant payment and invoicing conditions will be defined in each applicable "Statement of Work" (SoW). Client shall have Net 14 days from date of invoice to make payment. Client shall have Net 30 days from date of invoice to make payment on all invoices provided by Softway.

⚠️ Client Comment: PumpWorks AP processes invoices on a Net 30 cycle. Net 14 is not feasible with our internal approval workflow.

7. Warranty & Disclaimers

SOFTWAY WARRANTS ALL OF ITS SERVICES TO HAVE BEEN COMPLETED IN A GOOD AND WORKMANLIKE MANNER.

8. Limitation of Softway's liability

A. In no event shall Softway be liable to Client for lost profits of Client, or special or consequential damages, even if Softway has been advised of the possibility of such damages.

B. Softway's total liability, if any, under this Agreement for damages, costs and expenses, regardless of cause, shall not exceed the total amount of fees paid to Softway by Client under this Agreement or the sum of $5,000.00, whichever is greater. two times (2x) the total amount of fees paid to Softway by Client under this Agreement, or the sum of $10,000.00, whichever is greater.

⚠️ Client Comment: The $5,000 cap is commercially unreasonable for a $150,000 engagement. PumpWorks requires minimum 2x fee cap with $10k floor.

C. Softway shall not be liable for any claim or demand made against Client by any third party except to the extent such claim, or demand relates to copyright, trade secret, or other proprietary rights.

D. Client shall have the duty to mitigate its damages.

9. Relationship of the parties

Nothing contained herein will be construed as creating any agency, partnership, joint venture, or other form of joint enterprise between the parties. Softway is an independent contractor.

10. Non-solicitation of Employees

Parties agree not to hire or solicit other party's employees during the performance of this Agreement and for a period of 2 years after termination or expiration of this Agreement.

11. Mediation / waiver of jury

If a dispute arises under this Agreement, the parties agree to first try to resolve the dispute with the help of a mutually agreed-upon mediator in Houston or Harris County. Any costs and fees associated with the mediation shall be shared equally by the parties. In the event it becomes necessary for either party to file a lawsuit, the parties WAIVE THEIR RIGHT TO A TRIAL BY JURY.

12. Attorney fees

If any legal action is necessary to enforce this Agreement, the prevailing party shall be entitled to reasonable attorney fees, costs and expenses up to but not exceeding $5,000.00.

15. Governing law

This Agreement shall be interpreted under the laws of the State of Texas. Any and all legal actions relative hereto shall be in the courts of Harris County, Texas.

18. Term & Termination

This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and will remain in effect while any "SoW's" are outstanding. Either party may terminate this agreement by giving the other party thirty (30) days written notice of termination.
