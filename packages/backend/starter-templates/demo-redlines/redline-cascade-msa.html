MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement (the "Agreement") is made and effective on June 1, 2026.

BETWEEN: Cascade Financial Group (Hereafter referred to as the "Client"), a corporate entity organized and existing under the laws of the State of Illinois with its head office located at:

225 West Wacker Drive, Chicago, IL 60606

AND: Softway Solutions Inc. dba Culture+ (a Texas corporation) (Hereafter referred to as "Softway"), a company organized and existing under the laws of the State of Texas, with its head office located at:

1801 Main St. Houston, TX 77002

1. Client obligations: materials

Client shall cooperate and make available to Softway information, materials, facilities and equipment. The Client shall provide Softway within a reasonable amount of time all information, documentation, images, code or files requested by Softway and necessary for Softway to perform this Agreement.

3. Confidentiality

During the term of this Agreement and for 1 year 3 years afterward, Softway will use all Reasonable care to prevent the unauthorized use or dissemination of Client's confidential information. Reasonable care means at least the same degree of care Softway uses to protect its own confidential information from unauthorized disclosure.

⚠️ Client Comment: As a financial institution, Cascade Financial is subject to regulatory requirements mandating 3-year vendor confidentiality obligations under applicable banking regulations.

6. Payment

All relevant payment and invoicing conditions will be defined in each applicable "Statement of Work" (SoW).

All invoices are due within forty-five (45) days of receipt. Client will process payments via ACH only. Wire transfers will not be accepted. Any disputes regarding invoices must be raised in writing within 10 business days of receipt.

⚠️ Client Comment: Cascade Financial operates on a 45-day AP cycle mandated by our Treasury policy. ACH-only payment is required by our Finance department for all vendor disbursements over $50,000.

7. Warranty & Disclaimers

SOFTWAY WARRANTS ALL OF ITS SERVICES TO HAVE BEEN COMPLETED IN A GOOD AND WORKMANLIKE MANNER. SOFTWAY DISCLAIMS AND MAKES NO REPRESENTATION THAT THE SERVICES REQUESTED BY CLIENT ARE FIT FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE.

8. Limitation of Softway's liability

A. In no event shall Softway be liable to Client for lost profits of Client, or special or consequential damages.

B. Softway's total liability, if any, under this Agreement for damages, costs and expenses, regardless of cause, shall not exceed the total amount of fees paid to Softway by Client under this Agreement or the sum of $5,000.00, whichever is greater. the greater of: (i) the total fees paid to Softway by Client in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim, or (ii) $25,000.00.

⚠️ Client Comment: Our Legal and Risk teams require a minimum $25k floor and 12-month lookback for liability caps on all professional services agreements over $100k.

9. Intellectual Property

All intellectual property created by Softway Solutions under this Agreement shall remain the exclusive property of Client upon full payment of all fees.

All deliverables, work product, materials, and intellectual property created specifically for Client under this Agreement and fully paid for by Client shall become the exclusive property of Client upon final payment. Softway retains ownership of its pre-existing intellectual property, methodologies, tools, frameworks, and know-how ("Softway Background IP"), and grants Client a non-exclusive, perpetual, royalty-free license to use such Softway Background IP solely as incorporated into the deliverables.

⚠️ Client Comment: Cascade Financial's IP policy requires clarity on background IP vs. work product. We accept Softway retaining its methodology IP but need a perpetual license for any Softway tools embedded in our deliverables.

10. Non-solicitation of Employees

Parties agree not to hire or solicit other party's employees during the performance of this Agreement and for a period of 2 years 1 year after termination or expiration of this Agreement.

⚠️ Client Comment: 2-year non-solicitation is overly broad for a training services engagement. Cascade Financial's standard is 12 months.

15. Governing law

This Agreement shall be interpreted under the laws of the State of Texas. Any and all legal actions relative hereto shall be in the courts of Harris County, Texas. This Agreement shall be interpreted under the laws of the State of Illinois. Any and all legal actions relative hereto shall be in the courts of Cook County, Illinois.

⚠️ Client Comment: Cascade Financial is headquartered in Chicago, IL and requires Illinois governing law and jurisdiction for all vendor contracts.
